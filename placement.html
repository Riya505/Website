PLACEMENT


The College has a very active Career Guidance & Placement Cell (CGPC). It has established contacts with leading software and industrial houses for campus recruitment drives. The CGPC periodically organises placement training programmes and workshops for students to perform better in campus recruitments.
During 2019-20, a total of 65 jobs were offered by tier 1 companies like TCS, Infosys Wipro etc.to a maximum salary package of 4.2 lakhs CTC for the students of GEC Idukki.
